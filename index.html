Crystal Collector
Instructions
Each crystal is worth an unknown amount until you take one. The red, the blue, the green, and the clear. They are all worth something different each round.

Beware! If you take any more or any less than the allowed worth, this whole crystal cavern will collapse and you will either be impaled by a portruding crystal or crushed by the imploding cave.
Begin by clicking on a crystal.
